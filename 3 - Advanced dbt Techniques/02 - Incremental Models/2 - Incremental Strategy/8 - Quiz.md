Which of the following is not one of the five incremental strategies in dbt?

a. Append\
b. Merge\
c. Delete-insert\
d. Full-refresh

Answer: d

##
What incremental strategy is best suited for a data source that does not update historical records?

a. Append\
b. Merge\
c. Insert-Overwrite\
d. Delete-Insert

Answer: a

a. To process only new data and append it to the target table.\
b. To partition data by a unique key and process one partition at a time.\
c. To split a large dataset into smaller, more manageable chunks during incremental runs.\
